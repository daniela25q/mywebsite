Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish made of specially prepared sushi rice, combined with a variety of ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally fruits, that are usually raw or cooked. The sushi rice is seasoned with vinegar, sugar, and salt, and then shaped into bite-sized pieces or rolls. Sushi is usually served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side, and is commonly enjoyed as a snack or a light meal. It is a popular dish worldwide and has many variations and styles depending on the region and culture where it is prepared.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

4 servings

Ingredients

2 cups sushi rice

1/4 cup rice vinegar

2 cups water

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon salt

Sushi fillings (e.g. cucumber, avocado, crab meat, salmon, tuna)

Nori seaweed sheets

Instructions

step 1

Rinse the sushi rice with cold water until the water runs clear. Drain the rice and transfer it to a pot with 2 cups of water.

step 2

Bring the rice to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the rice cook for 18-20 minutes.

step 3

Mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small saucepan. Heat the mixture over low heat until the sugar and salt dissolve, then set it aside to cool.

step 4

Once the rice is cooked, remove it from the heat and let it sit for 10 minutes, covered. Then transfer the rice to a large bowl.

step 5

Pour the sushi vinegar over the rice and use a spatula to mix it in gently, taking care not to crush the rice grains.

step 6

Cut your desired sushi fillings into thin strips or small pieces.

step 7

Lay a sheet of nori seaweed on a sushi mat or a piece of plastic wrap.

step 8

Spread the rice evenly over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.

step 9

Add your sushi fillings on top of the rice in a line or in small pieces.

step 10

Roll the sushi tightly by using the sushi mat or plastic wrap to help you.

step 11

Repeat the process with the remaining nori sheets and rice until all the sushi is made.

step 12

Use a sharp knife to cut the sushi rolls into bite-sized pieces.

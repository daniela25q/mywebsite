Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made of a corn or wheat tortilla filled with various ingredients, such as meat, vegetables, cheese, and salsa. Tacos can be eaten as a main dish or as a snack, and they are often served with garnishes like cilantro, onions, and lime wedges.Tacos are often served with a variety of salsas and sauces, such as pico de gallo, guacamole, and hot sauce. They can also be topped with cheese, sour cream, and lettuce.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

29 min.

serving

4-6 servings

Ingredients

8-10 taco shells

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1/2 teaspoon paprika

1 teaspoon ground cumin

1 tablespoon chili powder

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 onion, diced

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 lb. ground beef

Instructions

step 1

In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.

step 2

Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

step 3

Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, until browned and cooked through.

step 4

Add the chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine and cook for another 2-3 minutes.

step 5

Warm the taco shells in the oven according to the package directions.

step 6

Assemble the tacos by filling each taco shell with the beef mixture and toppings of your choice.
